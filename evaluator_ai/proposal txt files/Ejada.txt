Analysis and Requirements EJADA will work with NCGR to understand the design model and
establish the project structures, reporting lines, staffing, sign off,

(Requirements Validation) project plans, dependencies, risks, issues and escalation etc. The
requirements of the solution will be documented in detail and
formally agreed between the delivery team and NCGR.

Architecture and Design The architecture and design of the solution will be documented and
formally agreed between parties. Functional and non-functional
requirements will be considered in the design process based around
the OOTB MDM Product 360 principles. The analysis will also extend
to help the NCGR gain an understanding of the source mapping to a
canonical format, understanding transformation rules, defining rules
within MDM for survivorship etc.

NCGR will be expected to take responsibility for the provision and
maintenance of the physical infrastructure for this project.

There will also be a gap analysis between OOTB MDM Product 360
functionality and further requirements, establishing a backlog for
possible future phases.

Data Provisioning and NCGR will be responsible for identifying all relevant source data

Mapping related to this engagement, reference data and both inbound and
outbound mappings in readiness for Data Quality provisioning and
delivery to the MDM Product 360 Hub

Build and Unit Test, EJADA will build and unit test the resultant MDM system.

System Integration Test and EJADA will pass the resultant MDM system to the Customer’s test

Performance Test team who will continue to carry out SIT and Performance testing
resulting in the handover of a completed system for User Approval
Testing by NCGR.

User Approval Testing NCGR will UAT test the resultant system. EJADA will provide support
to the testing process and will provide personnel to remedy any
issues identified where the solution does not meet the agreed
requirements and design.

Note that EJADA does not provide standard test cases for UAT
testing; we have found that UAT testing yields the best results if the
Customer’s test team bases their test protocols on the defined use
cases and expected business outcomes of the MDM delivery. EJADA
can however help the Customer ratify the test cases.

Deployment EJADA will support NCGR personnel to deploy the tested solution
into a production environment, NCGR will be responsible for the
actual production deployment. 53
